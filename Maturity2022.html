Maturity 2022

Maturity represents a dynamic fusion of faith, fun, and retro flair, brought to life through a vibrant design journey tailored for a college and young adult Christian summer retreat on the sun-kissed shores of South Padre Island, TX. Tasked with infusing the spirit of youthful energy and spiritual growth into every aspect of the project, I embarked on a creative exploration to craft a visual identity that would resonate with participants seeking both spiritual enrichment and seaside adventure.

At the heart of the design lies the logo, a playful homage to the iconic aesthetics of the 80s infused with a beachside vibe. Drawing inspiration from neon hues and sun-soaked shores, I curated a logo that captured the essence of Maturity in its retro-chic simplicity, inviting participants to embark on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual enlightenment against the backdrop of palm-fringed beaches and azure waters.

For the merchandise and stationery design, I delved further into the retro aesthetic, infusing bold colors, geometric shapes, and playful typography reminiscent of a bygone era. Each piece served as a vibrant canvas for the harmonious fusion of nostalgia and faith, creating a visual narrative that mirrored the dynamic and transformative experience awaiting retreat attendees as they embraced the journey towards maturity, both in spirit and in style.
