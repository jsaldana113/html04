Life in Christ

Life in Christ was a captivating project aimed at bringing the essence of faith and community to a young audience through design. Tasked with creating a cohesive visual identity for a high school and middle school Christian winter camp, I embarked on a journey to infuse vibrancy and modernity into every aspect of the project.

At the heart of the design was the logo, a symbol of unity and spirituality tailored to resonate with the youthful participants. Drawing inspiration from the camp's theme, I crafted a logo that blended contemporary aesthetics with timeless symbolism, capturing the essence of Life in Christ in a single emblem.

For the merchandise design, I opted for a sleek and stylish approach, incorporating cool colors like light blue and grey to evoke a sense of tranquility and serenity. The dark charcoal-colored shirts served as the perfect canvas for the logo, creating a striking visual impact that would resonate with camp attendees long after the event.

Completing the project was the stationery design, where every detail was thoughtfully considered to reflect the camp's mission of fostering connection and community. From letterheads to envelopes, each piece bore the signature Life in Christ logo, serving as a constant reminder of the transformative experience awaiting participants.
