Unmoved 2022
Unmoved stands as a testament to the unwavering strength of faith amidst the serene beauty of nature, brought to life through a captivating design journey tailored for a college and young adult Christian winter retreat nestled amidst the rustic charm of New Braunfels, TX. Charged with infusing the tranquility of the forest and the steadfastness of the mountains into every aspect of the project, I embarked on a creative odyssey to craft a visual identity that would resonate with participants seeking spiritual renewal and connection in the heart of the wilderness.
At the core of the design lies the logo, a powerful depiction of towering mountains standing firm against the passage of time. Drawing inspiration from earthy tones and natural textures, I curated a logo that captured the essence of Unmoved in its rustic simplicity, inviting participants to find solace and strength in the unshakeable foundation of their faith, much like the steadfast mountains that grace the horizon.
