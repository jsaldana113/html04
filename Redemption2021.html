Redemption 2021
“Redemption 2021” was an exciting and spiritually uplifting college summer retreat that was held in the picturesque setting of South Padre Island, Texas. This event aimed to provide a unique and transformative experience to college students, offering them a break from their routine and a chance for redemption, rejuvenation, and connection with their faith in a beautiful beach setting. The design was meant to capture the unique beauty of the beach, while maintaining a cool and bold look that young adults would be happy to wear year-round.
